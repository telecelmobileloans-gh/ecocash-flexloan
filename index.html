Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take?+
Most applications are approved within 15 minutes. Our instant verification system checks your eligibility in real-time, any time of day, including weekends.
Who is eligible for EcoCash Loans?+
Only active EcoCash subscribers are eligible. You need to be at least 18 years old with a registered EcoCash account and a verifiable income source. The longer your EcoCash history, the higher your eligible amount.
What is the maximum loan amount?+
You can borrow between USD 500 and USD 10,000 depending on your EcoCash subscriber history, income level, and credit profile. First-time borrowers may start at a lower limit which increases with good repayment behaviour.
What is the interest rate?+
A flat monthly rate of 8.9% applies to all loans. This is calculated on the principal for each month of your chosen repayment period. All amounts are shown upfront in the calculator — no surprises at repayment time.
How do I repay my loan?+
Repayment is automatic — the total amount is deducted from your EcoCash account on the due date. You can also make early repayments any time without any penalty fee.
Is my personal data secure?+
Yes. We use 256-bit bank-level encryption and strict security protocols. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
What happens if I miss a repayment?+
Missing a repayment may affect your future borrowing limit and incur a small late fee. We recommend ensuring sufficient funds in your EcoCash account before the due date. Contact support if you anticipate difficulty.
